What we do!

Diving into the Future of Aquaculture with our AI-Powered Robotics

All In One Solution

INNOROV's products include many features and tools combined in one single solution, therefore our clients would not need to buy many products to control their underwater assets.

Real Time Streaming

Since our ROV is remotely operated by human being, the operator has a real time stream in his screen where he can follow our ROV step by step into accomplishing all the tasks, in the same screen, our users can find other features like: Temperature, Dissolved oxygen and also water current.

DataBase Dashboard

Data about the fish's biomass is important to our clients. That is why, INNOROV provides a database dashboard where our clients can find their biomass calculus registered, each time they run processing algorithms.

About Us

Exploring Aquaculture's Future with INNOROV!

INNOROV solves subaquatic problems in offshore aquaculture, using Artificial Intelligence implemented in underwater robots.

Our mission is to transform the aquaculture sector by offering cutting-edge technological solutions to the problems associated with effective and sustainable fish farming. We enable fish farmers to make informed decisions, optimize feed management, and ultimately boost their yields while decreasing waste and environmental impact due to our software and user-friendly interface. Our objective is to develop a viable and sustainable future for aquaculture and help provide wholesome, sustainable fish for a growing global population.
